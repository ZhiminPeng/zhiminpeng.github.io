Zhimin Peng

I'm a Principal Applied Scientist at Amazon, where I work on search, recommendation, and agentic conversational AI systems for Fire TV. Before that, I did my PhD in Applied Mathematics at UCLA, focusing on large-scale optimization and its applications to machine learning.

I'm also the founder of Latent Value Lab, where I partner with small and mid-size companies to find the AI and ML opportunities in their business and build systems to capture them.

My work spans the full stack — from mathematical foundations to production systems at scale. I'm particularly interested in the gap between what ML can do in research and what it actually does in deployed products.
